# Google Form Setup Instructions

## 🚀 **How to Create the Form:**

1. **Go to Google Forms** (forms.google.com)
2. **Create a new form**
3. **Title**: "Business Website Customization Information"
4. **Description**: "Please provide the following information to customize your e-commerce website"

## 📝 **Form Questions to Add:**

### **Section 1: Company Information**

**Question 1: Company Name**
- Type: Short answer
- Required: Yes
- Question: "What is your company name?"

**Question 2: Business Type**
- Type: Multiple choice
- Required: Yes
- Question: "What type of business do you run?"
- Options:
  - Retail Store
  - Online Store
  - Wholesale
  - Service Business
  - Other (specify)

**Question 3: Industry**
- Type: Short answer
- Required: Yes
- Question: "What industry are you in? (e.g., Fashion, Electronics, Food, etc.)"

**Question 4: Target Market**
- Type: Multiple choice
- Required: Yes
- Question: "Who are your main customers?"
- Options:
  - Individual consumers (B2C)
  - Other businesses (B2B)
  - Both B2C and B2B

### **Section 2: Contact Information**

**Question 5: Business Address**
- Type: Paragraph
- Required: Yes
- Question: "What is your business address? (for shipping and returns)"

**Question 6: Phone Number**
- Type: Short answer
- Required: Yes
- Question: "What is your business phone number?"

**Question 7: Email Address**
- Type: Short answer
- Required: Yes
- Question: "What is your business email address?"

**Question 8: Business Hours**
- Type: Short answer
- Required: Yes
- Question: "What are your business hours? (e.g., Mon-Fri 9AM-6PM)"

### **Section 3: Branding**

**Question 9: Company Logo**
- Type: File upload
- Required: No
- Question: "Please upload your company logo (if you have one)"

**Question 10: Brand Colors**
- Type: Short answer
- Required: No
- Question: "What are your brand colors? (e.g., Blue and White, Red and Gold, etc.)"

**Question 11: Design Style**
- Type: Multiple choice
- Required: Yes
- Question: "What design style do you prefer?"
- Options:
  - Modern and Clean
  - Classic and Traditional
  - Bold and Colorful
  - Minimalist
  - Other (specify)

### **Section 4: Products**

**Question 12: Main Products**
- Type: Paragraph
- Required: Yes
- Question: "What products do you sell? (describe your main product lines)"

**Question 13: Product Categories**
- Type: Short answer
- Required: Yes
- Question: "How would you like to organize your products? (e.g., by type, by brand, by price range)"

**Question 14: Number of Products**
- Type: Multiple choice
- Required: Yes
- Question: "Approximately how many products do you have?"
- Options:
  - Less than 50
  - 50-200
  - 200-500
  - 500-1000
  - More than 1000

### **Section 5: Shipping & Delivery**

**Question 15: Delivery Areas**
- Type: Multiple choice
- Required: Yes
- Question: "Where do you deliver?"
- Options:
  - Local area only
  - National (within country)
  - International
  - Pickup only

**Question 16: Shipping Methods**
- Type: Checkboxes
- Required: Yes
- Question: "What shipping options do you offer?"
- Options:
  - Standard shipping
  - Express shipping
  - Same-day delivery
  - Pickup in store
  - Other (specify)

**Question 17: Free Shipping**
- Type: Short answer
- Required: No
- Question: "Do you offer free shipping? If yes, what's the minimum order amount?"

### **Section 7: Customer Service**

## ⚙️ **Form Settings:**

1. **Settings Tab:**
   - ✅ Collect email addresses
   - ✅ Limit to 1 response per person
   - ✅ Show progress bar
   - ✅ Shuffle question order: OFF

2. **Responses Tab:**
   - ✅ Accepting responses
   - ✅ Notify me of new responses

3. **Send Form:**
   - Copy the form link
   - Send to your employer via email

## 📧 **Email Template:**

**Subject:** Website Customization Information Needed

**Message:**
```
Hi [Employer Name],

To customize your e-commerce website with your business information, please fill out this form:

[FORM LINK]

This will help us:
- Set up your branding and colors
- Configure your products and categories
- Set up shipping and payment options
- Customize the website for your business needs

Please complete the form at your convenience. If you have any questions, feel free to ask.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

## 🎯 **Benefits of Using Google Forms:**

- ✅ **Easy to fill out** - User-friendly interface
- ✅ **Organized responses** - All data in one place
- ✅ **Professional appearance** - Looks official
- ✅ **Easy to share** - Just send the link
- ✅ **Automatic collection** - Responses saved automatically
- ✅ **Export data** - Can download as Excel/CSV
