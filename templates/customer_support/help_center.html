Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?
You can track your order by logging into your account and going to "Order History" or by using the tracking number sent to your email. We'll also send you updates when your order status changes.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Cash on Delivery (COD) for local orders. All payments are processed securely.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. Some items like electronics have a 14-day return window. Contact us to initiate a return.
How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 days) is available for an additional fee. We also offer same-day delivery in select areas.
Can I change or cancel my order?
You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it, as long as it hasn't been processed for shipping. Contact our support team immediately for assistance.
Do you offer international shipping?
Currently, we ship within Lebanon and select Middle Eastern countries. International shipping is available for certain products. Contact us for specific shipping options to your country.
